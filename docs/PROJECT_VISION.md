# EduArabic – Project Vision

## Mission
Empower Islamic educational institutions with a world-class, AI-assisted, offline-capable management platform that respects Islamic values and serves educators, students, and families across diverse communities.

## Vision
Be the leading SaaS platform for Islamic education management globally, serving madrasas, weekend Islamic schools, Quran academies, and full-time Islamic schools — starting in West Africa and expanding worldwide.

## Core Values
- **Amanah (Trustworthiness)** – Data privacy, security, and integrity in all operations
- **Ilm (Knowledge)** – AI-assisted tools that empower teachers, not replace them
- **Tarbiyah (Upbringing)** – Holistic tracking of student growth: academic, spiritual, and character
- **Ikhlas (Sincerity)** – Simple, honest UX that gets out of the way of learning

## Strategic Pillars
1. **Multi-Tenant SaaS** – One platform, unlimited institutions, complete data isolation
2. **AI-Assisted Teaching** – Question generation, Tajweed analysis, lesson creation — always teacher-approved
3. **Offline-First** – Works in low-connectivity environments across Africa, Asia, and beyond
4. **Parent Engagement** – Real-time visibility into child's academic and Hifz progress
5. **Hifz at the Core** – First-class Quran memorization tracking integrated throughout the platform

## Target Market
- **Primary**: Islamic weekend schools and Quran academies (Ghana, Nigeria, UK, US, Canada)
- **Secondary**: Full-time Islamic schools and madrasas
- **Tertiary**: Independent Quran teachers and tutoring circles

## Success Metrics
- 100+ institutions onboarded in Year 1
- 10,000+ active student profiles
- 95%+ offline attendance capture rate
- 4.5+ star App Store rating
- < 3s page load on 3G connection
